How to make Lasagna

To make a delicious lasagna, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  • Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Prepare Meat Sauce: In a pan, brown 1 pound of ground beef and 1 pound of Italian sausage with diced onions and garlic. Add tomato sauce and seasonings, simmer for a few minutes.
  • Mix Cheeses: In a bowl, combine 1 pound of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and some mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  • Layer Ingredients: In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then place lasagna noodles, followed by the cheese mixture. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce on top.
  • Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Remove foil, add more mozzarella and parmesan on top, and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  • Cool and Serve: Let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
